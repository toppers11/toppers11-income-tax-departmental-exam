Our Mission

Why We Built Toppers11

Every year, thousands of dedicated Income Tax Department employees appear for their departmental promotion examinations — MS, ITI and ITO. Yet for years, there was no dedicated online platform offering a realistic, paper-pattern mock test experience aligned to the CBDT-prescribed syllabus. Toppers11 was created to fill that gap.

100% Exam-Aligned

Every question bank and test series is built on the official Departmental Examination Rules, 2026 syllabus — no generic content, no filler. What you practice is exactly what appears in the exam.

Real Exam Interface

Our test player replicates the Online Mode exam interface — section-wise navigation, colour-coded question palette, mark-for-review, negative marking (1/8th) — so exam day feels familiar, not foreign.

AI-Powered Questions

Our 2,000+ question bank is curated from previous years' papers (2009–2025) and enhanced with AI-generated practice questions — covering every chapter, every weightage band across all exams.

By Department Insiders

Built by Income Tax professionals who have gone through the same examinations. We know the pain points — the service rule nuances, procedural questions, the allied law traps — and our tests reflect that insight.

Official Notification

Departmental Examination 2026 — Key Highlights

As per CBDT/HRD Directorate Notification dated 05.06.2026 (F. No. DE/Notification/ITO/ITI/MS/2026/ADG), the following schedule has been announced for the MS, ITO and ITI Departmental Examinations 2026, held under the new Departmental Examination Rules, 2026.

MS / ITO / ITI Departmental Examination 2026

Exam held in Online Mode (subjective papers — ITO Paper-IV, ITI Paper-V and MS Paper-V — recorded in physical answer booklets). Applications to be submitted online at www.incometaxindia.gov.in.
