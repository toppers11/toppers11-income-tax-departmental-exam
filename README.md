## What is the Income Tax Departmental Exam?

The **Income Tax Departmental Examination** is a promotion examination conducted by the **Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT)** for employees of the Income Tax Department of India.

### Purpose

Employees compete for promotions to higher positions:
- **Income Tax Inspector (ITI)** – supervisory role
- **Income Tax Officer (ITO)** – senior administrative position
- **Ministerial Staff / Upper Division Clerk (MS/UDC)** – administrative support role

### Exam Structure

- **Written examination** (objective + subjective questions)
- **Five papers** per exam category
- **500 total marks** across all papers
- **2–3 hours per paper** examination duration
- **CBDT syllabus** standardized across all candidates
