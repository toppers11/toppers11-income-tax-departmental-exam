Toppers11 is India's premier mock test platform designed exclusively for Income Tax Departmental Promotion Exams — MS (Ministerial Staff/UDC), ITI (Income Tax Inspector), and ITO (Income Tax Officer) — covering the official CBDT syllabus with 2,000+ questions including PYQs.

Toppers11 covers three Income Tax Departmental Promotion Exams as per CBDT Amended DE Rules 2009:

🔍 ITI (Income Tax Inspector) — 5 Papers · 500 Marks · Objective + Subjective
⚖️ ITO (Income Tax Officer) — 5 Papers · 500 Marks · Objective + Subjective · Includes 15 Case Laws
📂 MS/UDC (Ministerial Staff) — 4 Papers · 400 Marks · Descriptive + Objective · Books allowed in Paper 2 & 4

The MS/UDC exam has 4 papers of 100 marks each:

Paper 1 — Precis & Draft (Subjective, Without Books): Letter writing, AO notices, circulars, reopening of cases u/s 147/148, summons u/s 131(1).
Paper 2 — Office Procedure (Subjective, With Books): FR/SR rules, TA, Leave Rules, CCS Conduct Rules, GFR, Delegation of Financial Powers, HTC, Pension & GPF.
Paper 3 — IT Office Procedures: IT Act procedures, assessment, recovery, and departmental working.
Paper 4 — Practical Test (With Books): Applied office work based on IT procedures.

View full syllabus at MS Exam Syllabus.

The ITI exam has 5 papers of 100 marks each (total 500 marks), all objective type with 1/8 negative marking:

Paper I — Income Tax Law & Computation (150 MCQs, 3 Hrs): IT Act 1961, Wealth Tax, Black Money Act, Benami Act.
Paper II — Book-Keeping (100 MCQs): Accounting cycle, final accounts, bank reconciliation, depreciation.
Paper III — Allied Laws (100 MCQs): Transfer of Property Act, Registration Act, Succession Act, Hindu Law, Trusts Act.
Paper IV — Office Procedure: IT office procedures, assessment, TDS, recovery.
Paper V — General Knowledge & Current Affairs.

View full syllabus at ITI Exam Syllabus.

The ITO exam has 5 papers of 100 marks each (total 500 marks):

Paper I — IT Law & Computation (100 MCQs, Without Books): IT Act 1961 (Part A – 75 marks), 15 mandatory Case Laws (15 marks), Other Tax Laws incl. Wealth Tax, Black Money Act, Benami Act (Part B – 10 marks).
Paper II — Advance Accountancy (100 MCQs): Accounting standards AS-1 to AS-22, partnership accounts, company accounts, amalgamation.
Paper III — Allied Laws: Transfer of Property Act, Registration Act, Indian Succession Act, CPC, Indian Evidence Act.
Paper IV — IT Office Procedure: Assessment, appeals, TDS, penalties, recovery.
Paper V — General Knowledge.

View full syllabus at ITO Exam Syllabus.
